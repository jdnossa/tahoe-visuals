Request to book process

Clear expectations: request → review → confirm → pay → scheduled.

Every request enters our internal booking board as Pending. Management reviews timing, light, conditions, and availability. If approved, you receive a confirmation email with next steps. If not available, we suggest alternate times before you pay anything.

1
Request Submit your dates, session type, and details.
2
Review Management checks timing, conditions, and schedule.
3
Approved You receive confirmation + next steps (deposit link if required).
4
Scheduled Once confirmed, your session is locked in.

Request to book

Share your dates, group size, and the kind of session you’re imagining. We’ll reply with timing, location options, and a package recommendation that fits.

Requests are not confirmed until management approval. You won’t pay anything until your slot is approved.
